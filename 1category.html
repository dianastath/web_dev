In New York in 1957, a 16-year-old foreign boy is accused of parricide. His life is in the hands of 12 jurors who will decide if he is ultimately guilty and their decision must be unanimous. The 12 men, locked in a small room, sometimes clash and sometimes identify reflecting the prejudices of society through 12 crystal clear psychographs that unfold violently before the viewer's eyes.
